About GEC Palakkad

T Government Engineering College, Palakkad is a reputed engineering institution established by the Government of Kerala in 1999 and is situated in the calm and peaceful village of Sreekrishnapuram. It is approved by the AICTE and is affiliated to the University of Calicut and APJ Abdul kalam Technological University (from 2015 onwards). The college has five academic departments adequately staffed with faculties of national repute and a brilliant student community.
