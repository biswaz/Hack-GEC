Our Activities

OPEN DEBATE IEEE SB of GEC Palakkad organized an open debate with regards to wie wave 2.0, an initiative by IEEE LINK as a part of IWD to celebrate the achievements of women throughout history and across nations. Contention in argument of the topic, ‘Should there be reservation for women especially in the tech field?’ transpired on 9 March ’18 from 1 pm to 2 pm at the front entrance of the campus. About 17 participants including students from various departments as well as HODs and staff members ensured their vibrant presence and expressed their critical opinions during the course of the programme. The moderator was Ms. Kavya of ECE Dept. who was very dynamic in carrying the entire debate in a streamlined manner. The whole hearted commitment of the IEEE fraternity, the retric discourse of the participants and the lively audience contributed to the overall success of the event.
